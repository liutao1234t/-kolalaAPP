Kolala Community Guidelines & EULA Supplement

Last updated: June 2, 2026 · Contact: support@Kolala.fun
These guidelines apply to all user-generated content, challenge entries, community activity, videos, photos, comments, messages, and interactions on Kolala.

1. Community Purpose

Kolala is intended to be a positive cheerleading sports community where users can discover dance ideas, participate in challenges, share performance moments, and interact respectfully with other cheerleading enthusiasts.

All users must follow these guidelines in addition to Kolala’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others with respect. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, humiliation, threats, hateful conduct, targeted abuse, or repeated hostile behavior are prohibited.

Users may not attack others based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, medical condition, age, immigration status, veteran status, or other protected characteristics.

3. Cheerleading Safety

Cheerleading and dance activities may involve physical risk. Users must not encourage unsafe stunts, dangerous lifts, flips, tumbling, jumps, or routines without proper training, supervision, safe equipment, and adequate space.

Challenge content should not pressure users to perform dangerous movements beyond their ability. Kolala may remove content that appears to encourage unsafe behavior, reckless imitation, or physical harm.

4. Challenge Rules and Sportsmanship

Challenge participation should encourage creativity, teamwork, discipline, and positive competition. Users may not manipulate rankings, create fake engagement, submit stolen content, misrepresent team affiliation, or attack other participants.

Kolala may remove entries, adjust visibility, disqualify submissions, or restrict accounts where challenge activity appears unsafe, dishonest, infringing, abusive, or inconsistent with community standards.

5. Prohibited Content

Users may not upload, post, or share illegal, violent, hateful, sexually explicit, exploitative, fraudulent, defamatory, threatening, or abusive content.

Content involving minors must be handled with special care. Sexualized, exploitative, or unsafe content involving minors is strictly prohibited and may be reported to appropriate authorities where required.

6. Privacy and Personal Information

Users may not share another person’s private information without permission, including addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, IDs, financial information, private messages, school details, team rosters, location information, or private images.

Do not upload content containing people who did not consent to being shared where their privacy or safety could be affected.

7. Impersonation and Misrepresentation

Users may not impersonate athletes, coaches, teams, schools, creators, brands, organizations, or Kolala representatives. False claims of official affiliation, coaching credentials, awards, or team membership are prohibited.

8. Intellectual Property

Users should only upload videos, music, choreography, photos, logos, uniforms, team materials, or creative works that they own or are authorized to share.

Unauthorized use of copyrighted music, copied choreography videos, team recordings, school marks, or third-party content may result in removal and account enforcement.

9. Spam, Scams, and Platform Abuse

Fake accounts, artificial likes, automated follows, spam messages, scams, phishing, deceptive promotions, engagement manipulation, bots, scraping, or attempts to interfere with Kolala systems are prohibited.

10. Consumable Purchases and Fair Use

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with the Service rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, attempt unauthorized refunds, manipulate delivery of digital items, or use purchases to harass, pressure, or manipulate other users.

11. Reporting and Blocking

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, unsafe challenges, harassment, scams, impersonation, copyright violations, or other misuse. Blocking tools may be used to limit unwanted interactions.

Reports may be reviewed using automated tools, manual review, community signals, and moderation records.

12. Enforcement

Kolala may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, challenge disqualification, feature restrictions, purchase restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes.

Severity, repetition, harm level, legal requirements, and App Store compliance obligations may affect enforcement decisions.

13. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for their own posts, challenge entries, comments, messages, purchase activity, and interactions. You should evaluate sports content carefully before attempting any movement or routine.

By using Kolala, you agree to contribute positively and avoid behavior that harms users, creators, teams, or the platform.

14. Contact

Questions about these guidelines may be sent to support@Kolala.fun.
