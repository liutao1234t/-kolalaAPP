Kolala Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026 · Contact: support@Kolala.fun

This Privacy Policy explains how Kolala (“Kolala,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the Kolala mobile application, websites, cheer dance recommendation tools, challenge features, community functions, consumable in-app purchase features, and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

Privacy notice: Kolala does not include AI features and does not offer auto-renewable subscriptions. The Service may include community content, challenge participation, photos, videos, and consumable in-app purchases.

1. Who We Are

Kolala is a cheerleading sports and community platform. The Service may allow users to explore cheer dance recommendations, participate in cheerleading dance challenges, upload performance-related content, interact with community members, purchase consumable digital items, and access related sports community features.

For privacy questions, account deletion requests, data access requests, or other support matters, contact us at support@Kolala.fun.

2. Age Restriction

The Service is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe that a person under 18 has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to review and remove the information where required.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

We may collect information that you provide when you create an account, update your profile, upload content, join challenges, contact support, report content, make a consumable purchase, or otherwise interact with the Service.

  • Account and profile information: username, email address, profile photo, bio, age or date of birth, cheerleading interests, challenge preferences, saved content, and other information you choose to provide.
  • User content: cheerleading videos, dance challenge entries, photos, captions, comments, messages, reports, saved items, feedback, and other content you upload or submit.
  • Challenge information: challenge participation records, entry status, rankings, badges, rewards, votes, performance categories, and related interaction data.
  • Support and safety information: messages sent to customer support, report reasons, screenshots, moderation appeals, and information needed to investigate safety issues or enforce our Terms.
  • Purchase information: consumable product identifiers, receipt data, transaction identifiers, purchase country, entitlement status, and purchase validation information received from Apple or other payment platforms. We generally do not receive your full payment card number from Apple.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical data: device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, IP address, network information, crash logs, performance logs, diagnostic data, and device identifiers where permitted by law and platform rules.
  • Usage data: features used, pages viewed, dance categories browsed, challenges joined, videos watched, saved content, likes, follows, comments, search activity, purchase interactions, and general engagement events.
  • Security and fraud data: login records, abuse signals, moderation history, spam detection signals, device risk indicators, purchase risk signals, and logs used to detect unsafe activity, fraud, unauthorized access, or policy violations.

3.3 Device permissions

Some features require permission from your device. We only access these categories when you grant permission or use a feature that requires access.

  • Camera and photos: to upload profile images, cheerleading photos, dance videos, challenge entries, or other content.
  • Microphone: to record or upload videos with sound, voice, or audio elements if supported.
  • Notifications: to send account alerts, challenge reminders, activity updates, safety notices, and purchase-related information.
  • Contacts: only if we offer a feature that lets you find people you may know and you choose to enable that feature.

You can change permissions at any time in your device settings. Some features may stop working if permissions are disabled.

4. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Service: create accounts, display profiles, deliver cheerleading content, enable uploads, operate challenges, maintain saved items, process consumable purchases, and provide customer support.
  • To operate challenge features: manage entries, rankings, badges, participation records, reports, eligibility checks, reward distribution, and platform integrity systems.
  • To personalize the experience: recommend cheer dance categories, challenge ideas, creators, posts, teams, saved collections, and other content based on your interactions and preferences.
  • To process consumable purchases: validate purchase receipts, deliver digital items, prevent purchase fraud, maintain entitlement records, and respond to purchase support requests.
  • To keep the Service safe: detect fraud, spam, abuse, harassment, impersonation, unsafe content, dangerous challenge behavior, policy violations, and security incidents.
  • To communicate with you: send service messages, security alerts, challenge updates, product updates, support responses, policy notices, purchase confirmations, and other non-marketing communications.
  • To improve and develop the Service: analyze usage, debug errors, measure performance, test features, improve content ranking, and develop new services.
  • To comply with legal obligations: respond to lawful requests, enforce Terms, resolve disputes, protect rights, and satisfy tax, accounting, consumer protection, or platform requirements.

5. How We Share Information

We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With other users: profile information, public posts, challenge entries, comments, and other content you choose to make visible may be seen by other users.
  • With service providers: companies that help us operate the Service, such as hosting, cloud storage, analytics, customer support, crash reporting, payment verification, content moderation, security, fraud prevention, and infrastructure providers.
  • With app stores and payment platforms: to verify purchases, deliver consumable items, resolve purchase issues, and prevent fraud.
  • For legal and safety reasons: when necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, enforce Terms, investigate abuse, prevent harm, protect users, or protect the rights and security of Kolala and the public.
  • In business transfers: if Kolala is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction subject to appropriate protections.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense. If applicable law defines certain disclosures as a “sale” or “sharing,” we will provide rights and choices required by that law.

6. Consumable Purchase Data

Kolala may offer consumable digital items. If you purchase consumable items through Apple’s App Store, Apple processes the payment. We may receive purchase-related information such as product identifier, transaction identifier, purchase status, purchase date, country, receipt validation data, and delivery status.

We use purchase data to deliver consumable items, prevent fraud, maintain purchase records, handle support requests, and comply with legal or accounting requirements. Consumable items are not auto-renewable subscriptions and do not automatically renew.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. Retention periods may vary based on the type of information, the feature involved, legal requirements, safety needs, and whether your account remains active.

If you delete your account, we will take steps to delete or anonymize personal information, subject to reasonable delays and exceptions. We may retain certain information for limited periods to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent fraud, protect users, maintain security, preserve moderation records, or prevent banned users from immediately creating new accounts.

Some content you shared with other users, such as comments, messages, challenge entries, or content already viewed or saved by others, may remain visible to those users unless removed by the Service or required by law.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request portability of certain data.

  • You may update certain account and profile information in the app.
  • You may request account deletion through in-app controls where available or by contacting support@Kolala.fun.
  • You may revoke camera, photo, microphone, contacts, notification, or other device permissions through device settings.
  • You may opt out of promotional emails using unsubscribe instructions where available, although we may still send service, security, account, purchase, or policy messages.

For security, we may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests. We may reject or limit requests where permitted by law.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than where you live. Data protection laws in those countries may differ from those in your location. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers, such as contractual protections, technical safeguards, and organizational controls.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. These measures may include access controls, encryption in transit where appropriate, monitoring, internal policies, and security reviews.

No internet or mobile service can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential and using strong device and account security practices.

11. Cookies, SDKs, Analytics, and Similar Technologies

The Service may use cookies, SDKs, local storage, analytics tools, crash reporting tools, and similar technologies to operate the app, remember preferences, measure performance, prevent fraud, and improve user experience.

Some technologies may be provided by third-party service providers. Their data practices may depend on the tools integrated into the app. We aim to use such providers under appropriate safeguards and to reflect applicable data practices in app store privacy disclosures.

12. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take appropriate steps to delete it or otherwise handle it as required by law.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will revise the “Last updated” date. If changes are material, we may provide additional notice through the app, website, email, or other reasonable means.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions, requests, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our handling of personal information, contact us at support@Kolala.fun.
